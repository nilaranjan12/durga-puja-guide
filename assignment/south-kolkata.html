Welcome to South Kolkata Durga Puja

South Kolkata is known for its grand, theme-based Durga Pujas that attract millions of visitors each year. From stunning artistic pandals to cultural performances, every para celebrates the goddess with unmatched enthusiasm.

Deshapriya Park

Famous for unveiling the tallest Durga idol in Kolkata, Deshapriya Park Puja is a true spectacle of devotion and scale. The wide-open field becomes a sea of lights, colors, and spiritual energy every autumn.

Suruchi Sangha

Known for its creative pandal themes that represent different states of India, Suruchi Sangha Puja offers a unique cultural blend. The artistic presentation and social awareness themes make it a favorite among visitors.This famous puja pandal is famous for focusing on some unique theme. They always do something extraordinary to impress people coming from various parts of this world. The pandal is the winner of various awards like Idol, Theme, etc. Suruchi Sangha Club also participated in various social activities during the pandemic.

Santosh Mitra Square

Combining tradition with grandeur, Santosh Mitra Square (formerly Lebutala Park) showcases majestic idols and lavish decorations. Each year, the committee’s creative concepts attract devotees from across the city.

Ekdalia Evergreen

This puja dates back to several years, and is one of the most talked-about puja of South Kolkata.[1] Instead of modern themes, they prefer to present the traditional look. It focuses more on worshipping the Goddess Durga in the traditional way. Furthermore, the massive chandelier in the middle of the ceiling is the real attraction of this pandal. For most south residents, Ekdalia Evergreen Puja is an emotion.

Hindustan Park

Hindusthan Park is a highly popular pandal, presenting innovative themes every year. Moreover, it also organises various musical events, brings celebrities, and arranges other refreshments for the enthusiastic public. This puja has been attracting visitors since the old times of 1932. To date, it specialises in making innovative designs for the pandals. The Goddess on its Lion also gets different attires in the hands of the expert idol makers.

Jodhpur Park Durga Puja is renowned for its artistic pandals and intricately designed idols. The community’s dedication to preserving cultural heritage while embracing modern aesthetics makes it a standout celebration in South Kolkata.
