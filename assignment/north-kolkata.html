Welcome to North Kolkata Durga Puja

North Kolkata is the heart of heritage and tradition during Durga Puja. From century-old celebrations to modern artistry, every street lights up with joy, devotion, and creativity.

Bagbazar Sarbojonin

One of the oldest Durga Pujas in Kolkata, Bagbazar Sarbojonin has been a cultural icon for over a century. The traditional idol, classic lighting, and riverside location near the Ganga make it a must-visit.

College Square

Famous for its stunning reflection of the illuminated pandal on the lake, College Square Puja offers a magical evening view. It’s a hotspot for photographers and devotees alike.

Kumartuli Park

Located in the potters’ colony, Kumartuli Park Puja celebrates the very essence of idol-making. It showcases creative artistry and deep devotion, just steps away from where Durga idols are born.
